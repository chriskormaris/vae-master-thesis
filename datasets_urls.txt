You can download all the datasets one by one from these URLs:

* MNIST dataset:
http://yann.lecun.com/exdb/mnist

* Binarized MNIST dataset
https://github.com/yburda/iwae/tree/master/datasets/BinaryMNIST

* CIFAR-10 dasaset
https://www.cs.toronto.edu/~kriz/cifar.html

* OMNIGLOT dataset
https://github.com/yburda/iwae/tree/master/datasets/OMNIGLOT

* Cropped Extended Yale Faces Fatabase B dataset (maybe not valid)
https://vision.ucsd.edu/content/extended-yale-face-database-b-b

* The Database of Faces dataset
http://www.cl.cam.ac.uk/research/dtg/attarchive/facedatabase.html

* MovieLens 100K dataset
https://grouplens.org/datasets/movielens
